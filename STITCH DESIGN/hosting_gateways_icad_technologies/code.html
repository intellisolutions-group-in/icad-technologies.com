Frequently Asked Questions

What is the guaranteed uptime? expand_more

We provide a 99.99% uptime SLA across our global edge nodes, backed by financial credits if performance metrics fall below agreed thresholds.

How does the gateway handle DDoS attacks? expand_more

Our gateway includes L3/L4/L7 DDoS protection that automatically scrubs malicious traffic at the edge before it reaches your origin servers.

Can I use my existing SSL certificates? expand_more

Yes, you can upload custom certificates or use our automated managed certificate service powered by industry-standard CAs.

Where are your edge locations? expand_more

We operate over 200+ edge locations across all major continents, with high-density presence in APAC, EMEA, and North America.

Is custom caching logic supported? expand_more

Absolutely. We support Edge Scripting (JavaScript/Wasm) for complex caching rules, header manipulation, and dynamic routing.

How do you handle data residency requirements? expand_more

Customers can define specific regions for data storage and processing to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other local regulations.

Does the gateway support HTTP/3? expand_more

Yes, we provide full support for QUIC and HTTP/3 to ensure the lowest possible latency over mobile networks.

What kind of analytics are available? expand_more

Real-time log streaming, request/error rates, traffic by geography, and detailed cache-hit ratio reports are included.

How is billing calculated? expand_more

Billing is based on a flat infrastructure fee plus usage-based consumption (bandwidth and request volume).

Do you offer 24/7 technical support? expand_more

Enterprise plans include a dedicated Technical Account Manager and 24/7/365 priority access to our engineering support team.
